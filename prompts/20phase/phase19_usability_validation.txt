PHASE 19 OF 20: USABILITY VALIDATION
=====================================
Time Investment: Critical | Output: Verified Ease of Use

Verify the game is clear and usable for the target audience.

THE 10-YEAR-OLD TEST:
--------------------
For EACH screen:
- Can complete WITHOUT reading instructions?
- Is the ONE action obvious within 5 seconds?
- Are all interactive elements CLEARLY interactive?
- Is feedback IMMEDIATE and understandable?
- Is there ONLY ONE thing to do?
- Are touch/click targets at least 48px?

LANGUAGE CHECK:
- No jargon without explanation?
- Short sentences?
- Friendly, encouraging tone?
- "You discovered!" not "Correct"?
- "Try again!" not "Error"?

FORGIVENESS CHECK:
- Wrong actions prompt helpful hints?
- No harsh buzzers or negative feedback?
- No dead ends?
- Can replay to test ideas?

ENGAGEMENT CHECK:
- Self-paced (no forced timers)?
- Progress visible?
- Celebrations reinforce learning?
- Satisfying to complete?

THE EXPERT TEST:
---------------

SCENARIO AUTHENTICITY:
- Are scenarios authentic to real practice?
- Are numbers/values realistic?
- Would a professional recognize as accurate?
- Is terminology correct?

LEARNING DEPTH:
- Does it teach something experts might not articulate?
- Are causal mechanisms correct?
- Is detail level appropriate?
- Any misleading oversimplifications?

PROFESSIONAL VALUE:
- Would expert recommend to others?
- Does it capture real-world complexity?

FIX ALL USABILITY ISSUES:
------------------------
- Critical: Must fix (blocks completion)
- Major: Should fix (causes confusion)
- Minor: Can defer (polish items)

OUTPUT:
-------
Update comment block:

 *
