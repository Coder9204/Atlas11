PHASE 19: USABILITY TEST
Goal: Verify clarity and authenticity
Min to Proceed: Kid test passes
Uses: Phase 5 scenarios, Phase 8 specs

10-YEAR-OLD TEST (per screen):
□ ONE action obvious in 5 seconds
□ Can complete without reading instructions
□ Interactive elements clearly interactive
□ Feedback immediate and understandable
□ No confusion about what to do

LANGUAGE CHECK:
□ No jargon without explanation
□ Short sentences (≤12 words)
□ Friendly tone ("You discovered!" not "Correct")
□ Encouraging ("Try again!" not "Error")

FORGIVENESS CHECK:
□ Wrong actions get helpful hints
□ No harsh negative feedback
□ No dead ends
□ Can retry easily

EXPERT TEST:
□ Scenarios authentic (professional would recognize)
□ Numbers realistic
□ Terminology correct
□ Complexity appropriate

ENGAGEMENT:
□ Self-paced (no forced timers)
□ Progress visible
□ Satisfying to complete
