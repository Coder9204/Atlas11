PHASE 3: LEARNING ARCS
Goal: Map journey from misconception to mastery
Min to Proceed: 5+ concepts with stages 1-4
Uses: Phase 2 misconceptions, causal chains, variables

PER CONCEPT, DESIGN:
Q1: What surprising result challenges their wrong belief?
Q2: What do they manipulate to discover the pattern?
Q3: How does the WHY become visible?
Q4: What rule can they state after learning?

LEARNING ARC STAGES:
1. WRONG BELIEF: They think [Phase 2 misconception], predict [wrong outcome]
2. SURPRISE: [Q1 - unexpected result challenges them]
3. EXPLORE: They try [Phase 2 variables], notice [pattern]
4. REVEAL: They see [Phase 2 causal chain made visible]
5. CONSOLIDATE: They can state [Phase 2 insight as rule]
6. TRANSFER: New context where same principle applies
